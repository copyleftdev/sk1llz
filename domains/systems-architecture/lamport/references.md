# Leslie Lamport: References

## Essential Papers

### Time and Ordering
- **"Time, Clocks, and the Ordering of Events in a Distributed System"** (1978)
  - The foundational paper on logical clocks and happens-before
  - [ACM Digital Library](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/359545.359563)

### Consensus
- **"The Part-Time Parliament"** (1998)
  - The original Paxos paper (parliamentary metaphor)
  - [ACM Digital Library](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/279227.279229)

- **"Paxos Made Simple"** (2001)
  - Clearer explanation without the metaphor
  - [Microsoft Research](https://lamport.azurewebsites.net/pubs/paxos-simple.pdf)

### State Machine Replication
- **"The Implementation of Reliable Distributed Multiprocess Systems"** (1978)
  - State machine replication fundamentals

### Byzantine Fault Tolerance
- **"The Byzantine Generals Problem"** (1982, with Shostak & Pease)
  - Foundational work on BFT consensus
  - [ACM Digital Library](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/357172.357176)
